Seek Help Now

What is there to lose?

     A common reason for people refusing to seek help even when it's readily available in front of their faces is the mental health stigma. People don't want to be judged as weak or incompetant for seeking treatment. But why does that matter? Is that really more important than your own wellbeing? The reality is that you're not weak or incompetant for seeking help. In fact, going out and admitting that you need help is very courageous. If you or anyone you know is seeking help for a mental health or eating disorder, please join our discord community below. There is absolutely nothing to lose.
